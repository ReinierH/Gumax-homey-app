Control your Gumax veranda with Homey.

This app lets Homey take over your Gumax remote control, so you can operate your Gumax LED lighting and motorised sunshades from the Homey app, with voice assistants, or in your Flows.

LED SPOTS
Switch your veranda lighting on or off and choose from six brightness levels. Changes made with the physical remote are reflected in Homey, so the app always shows the actual state.

SUNSHADES
Open, close, or stop your motorised sunshading. Multi-channel remotes are fully supported: add each channel as its own device, or add the "all channels" button to control everything at once.

EASY SETUP
No hubs or extra hardware needed — Homey's built-in 433 MHz radio does all the work. During setup, simply press a button on your existing remote and Homey learns it automatically. Your remote keeps working alongside Homey.
